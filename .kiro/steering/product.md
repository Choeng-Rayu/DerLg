# Product Overview

DerLg.com is a Cambodia travel booking platform with an AI-powered travel concierge. The platform serves tourists (English, Chinese, Khmer speakers) visiting Cambodia, providing trip planning, transportation, hotels, tour guides, and emergency services.

## Core Value Proposition

Mobile-first PWA that combines traditional booking with conversational AI to help travelers discover, plan, and book complete Cambodia trips through natural language chat.

## Key Features

- AI Travel Concierge (conversational booking via WebSocket)
- Transportation booking (tuk-tuk, van, bus)
- Hotel and tour guide booking
- Trip discovery and smart suggestions
- Emergency and safety system
- Student discount verification
- Loyalty points program
- Offline maps and multi-language support (EN/ZH/KM)
- Festival calendar and event alerts

## Target Users

- International tourists visiting Cambodia
- Chinese tourists (primary market)
- Students seeking discounted travel
- Travelers needing emergency assistance in unfamiliar areas

## Business Model

Commission-based on bookings (transportation, hotels, guides) with premium features for loyalty members.
